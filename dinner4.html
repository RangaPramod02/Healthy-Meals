potato and pea smash

A healthy and nutritious meal packed with flavor and nutrients!

Ingredients

  • 800g packet frozen traditional roast potatoes
  • 1/2 x 500g packet frozen cauliflower florets
  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1 brown onion, chopped
  • 1 1/2 tbsp rogan josh curry paste
  • 3/4 cup frozen peas
  • 165ml can coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh coriander, plus extra sprigs to serve

Recipes

  1. Place frozen potato and cauliflower in a large saucepan of boiling water. Boil for 5 minutes or until just tender.
  2. Meanwhile, heat oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Cook onion for 3 to 4 minutes or until soft. Add curry paste. Cook for 1 minute or until fragrant.
  3. Drain potato and cauliflower. Add to frying pan with frozen peas. Cook for 2 minutes, pressing vegetables with the back of a spoon to smash. Season with salt and pepper. Add coconut milk. Stir to combine. Cook for 1 minute or until heated through. Remove from heat. Stir in coriander. Serve smash topped with coriander sprigs.

Nutritional Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 420
  • Protein: 8g
  • Carbs: 63g
  • Fat: 17g
